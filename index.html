Our Eyelash Services

Every woman’s eyes tell a different story. Whether you’re after a natural, everyday glow or full-volume glamour, we tailor our services to match your unique style. Our team takes the time to consult with you, understanding your preferences and designing lashes that fit your personality and lifestyle.

Classic Eyelash Extensions

For a timeless, elegant look, our classic lash extensions are perfect. We apply a single, silky extension to each of your natural lashes, creating a refined and natural enhancement that adds subtle definition to your eyes.

Volume Eyelash Extensions

If bold and beautiful is your vibe, our volume lashes will give you that stunning, dramatic flair. We apply lightweight fans of multiple extensions to each lash, offering a lush, full appearance that still feels weightless.

Hybrid Eyelash Extensions

Want the best of both worlds? Our hybrid lashes blend classic and volume techniques, giving you a textured, eye-catching look that’s both chic and versatile.

Mega Volume Eyelash Extensions

For maximum fullness and an ultra‑glam look, mega volume lashes feature even denser fans for that bold, statement‑making style.

Lash Lifts & Tints

For clients who prefer to enhance their natural lashes, our lash lift and tint services are the ideal choice. A lash lift curls your lashes upward, opening your eyes and adding length, while a tint deepens their colour for a mascara-free finish.

Lash Fills & Maintenance

Maintaining your lash extensions is key to keeping them looking flawless. We recommend lash fill appointments every 2 to 3 weeks, where we refresh your lashes and replace any that have naturally shed.

Preparing for Your Lash Appointment

To make the most out of your lash appointment, here are a few tips from our lash artists:

  • Come with Clean Lashes: Avoid wearing mascara or eyeliner on the day of your appointment. This allows for better adhesive bonding. 
  • Limit Caffeine Intake: Caffeine can make your eyes flutter, which may affect the precision of the lash application. 
  • Schedule with Relaxation in Mind: Lash extensions take time and precision. Use this as your chance to relax and enjoy some quiet pampering. 
  • Bring Your Style Inspiration: If you have a particular lash look in mind, share photos with us. We’ll customize the look to suit your features perfectly.

How to Keep Your Lashes Looking Their Best

We’ll guide you on how to care for your lashes after the service to maintain their beauty and longevity. Here are some aftercare essentials:

  • Avoid oil-based makeup removers and skincare products near the eyes. 
  • Gently cleanse your lashes with a lash-safe cleanser to keep them clean and fluffy. 
  • Use a lash brush daily to maintain their shape and prevent tangling. 
  • Schedule regular fills to keep your lashes looking their best.

We’re always here to answer your questions, offer tips, and ensure you feel fully equipped to maintain your beautiful lashes.
